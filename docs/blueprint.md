# **App Name**: Castro Nepal

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog & Display: Browse and view digital game redeem codes and gaming products in a dynamic, gaming-themed grid layout across the homepage, products page, and detailed product views. Includes essential product information such as images, names, prices, descriptions, categories, and stock status.
- Intuitive Product Search & Filtering: Users can efficiently locate products using a search bar with auto-suggestions, and refine their choices with category and price filters, or by sorting products by popularity and recent additions.
- Secure User Authentication System: A comprehensive login and signup system supporting Google Account authentication and manual registration via email and password. Registered users gain access to a personal dashboard showcasing order history and saved contact information.
- Discord Webhook Order Processing: A streamlined checkout process where customers provide necessary details, and upon submission, order data is automatically sent to a specified Discord webhook, followed by a success confirmation.
- Product Management Admin Panel: A hidden, password-protected admin dashboard enabling administrators to add, edit, delete products, manage inventory (stock status), upload product images, set prices, and update descriptions.
- Announcement System: An administrative tool allowing the creation and posting of announcements, such as new redeem code availability or discounts, which are prominently displayed on the website's homepage to notify users.
- AI-Powered Product Suggestion Tool: Leverages AI to provide smart product recommendations, such as trending items or related products, enhancing product discoverability and user engagement across the site.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary accent color: A vibrant, electric blue (#1A80E6) to signify interactivity and a futuristic, premium gaming experience, standing out on dark backgrounds.
- Background color: A deep, immersive blue-gray (#14181B) providing a sleek, dark canvas for the entire user interface, enhancing the neon glow effects.
- Secondary accent color: A glowing cyan (#1ADFFF) to introduce dynamic contrast and 'neon glow' effects, aligning with the futuristic and gaming-themed aesthetic.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), chosen for its computerized, techy feel that perfectly complements the futuristic gaming theme.
- Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), providing a clean, modern, and highly legible text experience for product descriptions and general content, pairing well with the headline font.
- Abstract, minimalist icons with an optional 'neon glow' treatment, echoing the site's gaming-style UI panels and futuristic design principles.
- The layout will heavily feature 'Glassmorphism' effects and distinctive 'Gaming-style UI panels' for all content modules, ensuring a premium feel and full responsiveness across all devices (mobile and desktop).
- Emphasize smooth and subtle animations, including page transitions, product card interactions, hover effects, and a custom loading animation, to deliver a highly dynamic and responsive user interface.
